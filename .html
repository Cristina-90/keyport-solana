THE PROBLEM

Market Inefficiency

Millions of digital nomads, international students, and professionals face housing barriers when relocating. This creates massive friction in global mobility and labor markets.

Reputation Doesn't Travel

When tenants move internationally, their rental history remains locked in local systems. Every relocation requires starting from zero, regardless of years of perfect payment records.

No Trust, No Access

Landlords lack reliable verification methods for foreign tenants. Without verifiable history, they reject qualified candidates or demand excessive security deposits to protect themselves.

Your Data, Their Control

Platforms like Airbnb and credit agencies own your reputation data. You cannot export it, and they can delete or monetize it without your consent.

THE SOLUTION

A decentralized reputation protocol built on Solana blockchain

[01]

Unforgeable Proof

Paper contracts can be faked or lost. NFTs on Solana create permanent, tamper-proof records that both parties signed and completed successfully.

[02]

On-Chain Attestations

Leveraging Solana's compressed accounts for 1000x cost reduction while maintaining full transparency. Each rental = cryptographic proof, permanently recorded.

[03]

Protected Identity

Zero-knowledge proofs protect your personal data. Prove you're reliable without exposing passport numbers, addresses, or income details.

[04]

Never Lost, Never Destroyed

Unlike paper documents that can burn, flood, or disappear, your blockchain reputation is permanent and accessible from any device, anywhere.

[05]

Two-Way Accountability

Both sides build reputation. Bad landlords get exposed, unreliable tenants can't hide. The system creates mutual trust through transparency.

[06]

Truly Yours

Your reputation lives in your wallet, not on a company's server. No one can delete your history when you switch platforms or countries.
